About

Our website aims to help users track and understand their income, expenses, and savings effectively.

Developed by Vishnupriya, Amrutha, and Saana, three BTech CSE students from GISAT College, this website serves as their mini project.

The user data is collected and stored locally on the browser's localStorage only.

Credits to the creators and GitHub for hosting this website.

We are continuously working to add more features to enhance your experience.
